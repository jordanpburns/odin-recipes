Vegan Gravy

Description

Seems like a pretty standard vegan gravy to me. I'm going to add mushrooms to this recipe becuase mushrooms are dank. Source: Vegan Fatty on AllRecipes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons tamari sauce
  • 2 cups soy milk
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • ground black pepepr to taste
  • 6-8 medium brown mushrooms sliced

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. let mushrooms brown for 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk flour, vegetable broth and tamari into hot oil; cook and stir until there are no lumps and the mixture becomes paste-like, about 5 minutes
  3. Pour soy milk into the skillet;cook and stir until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add nutritional yeast and black pepper; stir.
