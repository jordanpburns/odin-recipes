Vegan Coconut Coffee Ice Cream

Description

The allrecipes description of this recipe is rather sparse. It reads: "A tasty, low carb, vegan ice cream. Enjoy!" The picture above isn't even from the all recipes page; it's taken from lovingitvegan. I suppose we'll just have to let this vegan ice cream recipe speak for itself.

Ingredients

To be honest I don't really like this recipe so I've made some changes from the original.

  • 1 14 ounce can of cocnut milk
  • 1 14 ounce can of coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground espresso beans
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Stir cocnut milk, cocnut cream, brown sugar and white sugar together in a saucepan. Cook on medium low heat until sugar dissolves. Add coffee powder cocoa and vanilla extract. Whisk until fully combined.
  2. Remove 2 tablespoons of the coconut mixture. Whisk with cornstarch in a bowl to make a slurry. Pour back into the coconut mixture. Stir until slurry is evenly distributed.
  3. Refridgerate until completely chilled, 3 hours to overnight
  4. Freeze 8 hours to overnight
