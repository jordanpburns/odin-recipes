Vegan Pesto

Description

According to allrecipes.com, where I found this recipe, this vegan pesto is a classic recipe that the author uses and loves. The main difference between this recipe and a non vegan pesto is the use of nutritional yeast rather than parmesean cheese. I have actually made a similar recipe and it is very good on pastas, bread, sandwhiches, etc.

I also reccomend finishing off a dish with sundried tomatoes in addition to this great vegan pesto. This recipes makes 16 servings. Source: CANDIEDVIOLET on allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 2/3 cup olive oil
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the pine nutes in a skillet over medium heat. Stir constantly until lightly toasted.
  2. Gradually mix in the pine nuts, olive olive, nutritional yeast and basil in a food processor and process until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
